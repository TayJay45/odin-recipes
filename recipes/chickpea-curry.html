Chickpea Curry

Description

This is a bowl of chickpea curry. One of my favorite recipes to make. It is easy, delicous and nutritious.

Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas
  • onions
  • tomatoes
  • garlic
  • coconut milk

Steps:

  1. Dice onion, garlic, heat up wok with oil
  2. Sautee onion and garlic until soft
  3. Add salt and pepper to wok
  4. Add tomatoes and spices to wok
  5. Add chickpea and coconut milk to wok and let simmer
