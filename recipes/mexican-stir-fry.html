Mexican Stir Fry

Description:

Stir fry is also one of my favorite things to eat. All you do is take a bunch of veggies, like mushrooms, carrots, brocoli, onions and bell peppers and sautee them with Mexican style spices. These spices include, paprika, oregano, cumin etc.

Ingredients:

  • onions
  • tomatoes
  • garlic
  • bell peppers
  • brocoli

Steps:

  1. Take all veggies and chop them up to about the size of a dime
  2. Sautee onions and garli until soft
  3. Throw the rest of the veg in with the spices and cook until al dente
